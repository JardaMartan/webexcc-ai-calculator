Agent-facing Webex AI is billed as consumption Units (not per seat): 1 Unit = 1,500 covered agent-voice minutes or 1,000 covered digital sessions, rounded up. The coverage below is taken from the scenario — enable AI Assistant and/or AI QM on the flow diagrams; enabling both applies the discounted bundle price. AI capability matrix

What each provider can do across the flow roles (scripted agent, autonomous/LLM agent, agent assistant, QM/topic analytics) and the supporting capabilities. Use it to pick the right AI for each touchpoint.

Within Webex Contact Center, Agent Assistant (aware of the live customer conversation), Real-time transcript and Topic detection are provided exclusively by Webex AI Assistant, and Quality Management exclusively by Webex AI QM. Other AIs can only act as “chat with the knowledge base” — they don’t have the context of the current customer conversation.
